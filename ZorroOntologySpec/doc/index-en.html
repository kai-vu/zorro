Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

We developed an upper-level ontology for fault diagnosis in cyber-physical systems that captures the essential concepts and relations required for efficient diagnostic. The ontology was created through analysis of public and proprietary fault datasets, consultations with domain experts from two industrial partners, and a review of existing reference ontologies such as IDO and IOF-MRO. By modeling both operational and functional dependencies, often overlooked in prior work, Zorro enables system-level causal reasoning and better representation of failure propagation.
